About ME

Hello there! I'm Padmank and I love learning new things, meeting new people, and overcoming new challenges

Currently, I am a student at Rutgers University. At Rutgers, I study the ever evolving field of computer science. Prior to that, I was a part of the Math and Science Magnet Program at Morris Hills High School. It was in high school that I began my coding journey. I started experimenting with HTML and CSS and was eventually able to create simplistic webpages(unlike the one you are currently visiting). Eventually, after taking AP Computer Science, I began to learn more advanced object oriented languages, namely Java and Python. It was also around this time that I started developing rudementary android apps, some of which are shown in the "projects" section.

Unlike the typical computer science major, I do have other interests, though they are quite nerdy. I am an ardent fan of Jeopordy and have watched the recent "Greatest of All Time" tournament religiously. In terms of activities, provided the weather is not unbearable, I enjoy embarking on long hikes accross local trails. On a similar note, I also delight in long drives accross open roads.

Throughout my life I have relished new challenges, whether they be physical of mental. My biggest, albeit my only, athletic accomplishment was earning my first degree black belt in taekwondo in November of 2016. Through an arduous 7 year journey and a grueling 3 day testing process, I emerged with a strong sense of self-confidence and a black belt in hand. In addition to that, I also take pride in the fact that I graduated Morris Hills High School with an AP Capstone Diploma earned through authoring a formal research paper. Though the process of conducting research was cumbersome, I ended up producing a product that I could be proud of. The final high point in my life occured during the summer of 2019, where I participated in a significant religious function. The function was my "Thread Ceremony" which officially initiated me into Brahminism. Below you will find a few pictures detailing my major accomplishments.

My ML Project

Here is a view of an exciting machine learning project I created. The program learns to succeed at Google Chrome's hidden dinosaur runner game which appears when there is no internet. Using TensorFlow.js, an AI is built and trained through a process called neuroevolution in order to progressively improve at the game. The program beings by generating 200 dinosaurs which move about randomly. The players who get the furthest are chosen to pass their genetic material, or in other words, the weights of their nueral network, onto the next generation of dinosaurs. This process is continued until a player has evolved enough to never die.
